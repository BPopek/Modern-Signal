Broken IFAP Links:

Due to site-wide changes on the Information for Financial Aid Professionals (IFAP) website in February, links from Knowledgebase Q&As may be broken. We are working to reconnect these as soon as possible. In the meantime, if you need to find the referenced documents, please visit the IFAP home page and navigate to the intended item.

Revised Term Lengths:

In the November 5, 2019 Electronic Announcement, Revised Policy for Standard Term Length, ED revised the definitions of standard terms. This guidance became effective immediately on the date of posting. As you review Q&As within the AskRegs Knowledgebase, please consider the answers within the context of ED’s new guidance and examples. We will be updating affected Knowledgebase Q&As over the coming weeks.
